# Ferrari SF-23

The Ferrari SF-23 is a Formula One racing car designed and constructed by Scuderia Ferrari for the 2023 Formula One World Championship. Despite high expectations, the car struggled with performance issues throughout the season.

## Technical Specifications

### Power Unit
- **Engine**: Ferrari 066/10 V6 turbo hybrid
- **Power Output**: Competitive hybrid system
- **Turbocharger**: Advanced compressor technology
- **Energy Recovery**: MGU-K and MGU-H integration

### Chassis Design
- **Monocoque**: Carbon fiber construction with halo
- **Aerodynamics**: Conservative approach focusing on stability
- **Suspension**: Traditional Ferrari suspension layout
- **Weight**: 798 kg minimum weight

### Electronics and Control
- **ECU**: Ferrari-developed control systems
- **Data Systems**: Advanced telemetry and analysis
- **Sensors**: Comprehensive vehicle monitoring

## 2023 Season Performance

The SF-23 faced significant challenges:

- **Wins**: 0
- **Podiums**: 3
- **Pole Positions**: 1
- **Points**: 406 (4th in constructors)

## Development Issues

### Balance Problems
The car suffered from inconsistent balance, making it difficult for drivers to extract maximum performance.

### Aerodynamic Efficiency
Conservative aerodynamic approach didn't match the pace of top teams like Red Bull and Mercedes.

### Power Unit Reliability
While powerful, the engine had occasional reliability issues affecting race results.

## Driver Adaptation

Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz showed remarkable skill adapting to the car's limitations:

- Leclerc: Fought for wins despite car shortcomings
- Sainz: Consistent points scorer, strong qualifying

## Key Features

### Traditional Ferrari DNA
The SF-23 maintained Ferrari's traditional design philosophy with smooth lines and elegant aesthetics.

### Advanced Materials
Use of cutting-edge carbon fiber and titanium components for weight reduction and strength.

### Hybrid System Integration
Sophisticated integration of electrical and mechanical power systems.

## Lessons Learned

The SF-23 experience taught Ferrari valuable lessons for the SF-24 development, focusing on improved aerodynamics and car balance.

## Future Outlook

Ferrari's 2024 car, the SF-24, incorporates lessons from the SF-23 with improved performance and competitiveness.

## Legacy

While not a success story, the SF-23 highlighted Ferrari's engineering capabilities and determination to return to winning ways.
