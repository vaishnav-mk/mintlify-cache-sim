# Max Verstappen

Max Emilian Verstappen (born 30 September 1997) is a Belgian-Dutch racing driver competing in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. He is the reigning three-time Formula One World Champion and the youngest driver to win a Grand Prix.

## Early Career

Born in Hasselt, Belgium, Verstappen began karting at age 4. He progressed rapidly through the junior formulas, winning the European Formula 3 Championship in 2014.

## Formula One Journey

### Toro Rosso (2015-2016)
Verstappen made his F1 debut at age 17, becoming the youngest driver in F1 history. He scored points in his first race and achieved his first podium in Spain.

### Red Bull Racing (2016-present)
Joining Red Bull in 2016, Verstappen became the youngest pole sitter and race winner. His aggressive driving style and raw speed made him a fan favorite.

## World Championships

- **2021 World Champion**: Won 10 races, dominated the season
- **2022 World Champion**: Clinched title with 4 races remaining
- **2023 World Champion**: Third consecutive title, equaling legends like Prost and Piquet

## Driving Style

Known for his fearless approach and ability to extract maximum performance from any car. His overtaking maneuvers and defensive driving are legendary.

## Records

- Youngest pole position (17 years, 166 days)
- Youngest race winner (17 years, 180 days)
- Youngest World Champion (24 years, 73 days)
- Most consecutive wins in a season (10 in 2023)

## Personal Life

Verstappen is known for his love of gaming and sim racing. He maintains a close relationship with his family and girlfriend Kelly Piquet.

## Future Goals

At 26, Verstappen shows no signs of slowing down. He aims to break more records and continue Red Bull's dominance in F1.
