# Charles Leclerc

Charles Marc Hervé Perceval Leclerc (born 16 October 1997) is a Monégasque racing driver competing in Formula One for Scuderia Ferrari. Known for his smooth driving style and precision, he is widely regarded as one of the most talented drivers of his generation.

## Early Career

Born in Monte Carlo, Monaco, Leclerc grew up idolizing Formula One legend Ayrton Senna. He began karting at age 8 and progressed through the junior formulas.

### Formula Renault 2.0 (2014)
Won the Northern European Cup championship.

### GP3 Series (2016)
Finished 2nd in the championship with 3 wins.

### FIA Formula 2 (2017)
Won the championship with 5 wins, becoming the first rookie to do so.

## Ferrari Journey

### Sauber/Ferrari (2018-present)
Leclerc joined Alfa Romeo Sauber F1 Team in 2018, becoming Ferrari's official test driver. In 2019, he moved to the main Ferrari team as Kimi Räikkönen's replacement.

## Achievements

- **5 Grand Prix Victories**
- **26 Podium Finishes**
- **26 Pole Positions**
- **Fastest Lap Awards**: 8

## 2019 Season Highlights

Leclerc's debut season with Ferrari was spectacular:
- First win at Spa-Francorchamps (youngest Ferrari winner)
- Multiple podiums
- Strong qualifying performance
- Finished 4th in championship

## 2022 Season

Despite Ferrari's car struggles, Leclerc showed immense talent:
- Won in Australia and Austria
- Fought for the championship until the final race
- Finished 2nd in the drivers' championship

## Driving Style

Leclerc is known for his smooth, precise driving and ability to maximize any car's potential. His feedback to engineers is highly valued.

## Personal Life

Leclerc is close to his family and maintains strong relationships with his brother Lorenzo and sister Charlotte. He enjoys karting with friends and supports various charities.

## Future at Ferrari

At 26, Leclerc is Ferrari's golden boy. With improved cars, he aims to challenge for championships and bring glory back to Maranello.
