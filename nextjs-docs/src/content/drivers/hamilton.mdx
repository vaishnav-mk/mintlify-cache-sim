# Lewis Hamilton

Lewis Carl Davidson Hamilton MBE HonFREng (born 7 January 1985) is a British racing driver who races in Formula One for Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team. A seven-time Formula One World Champion, he is often regarded as one of the greatest drivers in the history of the sport.

## Career Highlights

- **7x World Champion** (2008, 2014, 2015, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020)
- **103 Grand Prix Victories** (most in F1 history)
- **104 Pole Positions** (most in F1 history)
- **197 Podium Finishes**

## Early Life

Born in Stevenage, Hertfordshire, Hamilton showed early promise in karting. He won the British Kart Championship at age 10 and progressed through the junior ranks, winning multiple championships.

## Formula One Debut

Hamilton made his F1 debut with McLaren in 2007, becoming the first black driver in the sport. He finished his rookie season with 4 podiums and was named Rookie of the Year.

## Mercedes Era

Joining Mercedes in 2013, Hamilton formed a dominant partnership with the team. The Mercedes W05 hybrid car, combined with Hamilton's driving skill, led to his second world title in 2014.

## Records and Achievements

Hamilton holds numerous F1 records:
- Most career points
- Most career wins
- Most career pole positions
- Most career podiums
- Youngest driver to reach 100 wins

## Philanthropy

Beyond racing, Hamilton is known for his environmental activism and philanthropy. He founded the Mission 44 charity and advocates for diversity in motorsport.

## Personal Life

Hamilton enjoys a close relationship with his family and maintains a private personal life despite his fame. He has homes in Monaco, Switzerland, and the United States.
